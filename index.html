📚 How to Use This Guide
"J" sound: Pronounce like "zh" in "measure" (soft, not hard J).
"R" sound: From the throat (guttural). Not like English R—think gargling slightly.
Silent letters: Final consonants often aren't pronounced. If it's at the end, you probably don't say it.
Nasal vowels: "on", "an", "in", "un"—pronounce slightly through your nose. Very French!
